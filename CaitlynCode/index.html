Do you feel comfortable talking about your IEP?

If someone is trying to get in my business then no. but if it's like an interview or a job or college then yea i would have to be comfortable and have to deal with it. If someone just came up to me about it then yea i would be very uncomfortable

Do you know you have an IEP?

Kinda do. I honestly don't know the full details but I do know the basics of it. For example, you get an accommodations like extended time for test or extended time for homework. Sometimes the teacher gives me the shorter version of the test than the original.
